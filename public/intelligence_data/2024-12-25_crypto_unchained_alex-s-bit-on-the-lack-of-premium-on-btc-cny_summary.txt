Key Insight
Contrary to popular belief, Bitcoin is not a primary method for capital outflow from China, with tether and other means being more prevalent for moving money out of the country.
<list_of_important_information>
- There is rarely a premium on Bitcoin/CNY OTC market trades
- Tether is perceived as a more accessible store of value in China compared to Bitcoin
- Local Chinese exchanges are considered difficult to use
- Dollars are more universally understood as a store of value
- Alternative methods of capital outflow from China exist beyond Bitcoin
</list_of_important_information>
