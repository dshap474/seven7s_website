Key Insight
2025 is expected to be a year of significant global macro changes with complex implications for asset markets, characterized by potential opportunities and risks driven by global refinancing needs and macroeconomic shifts.

<list_of_important_information>
- Global liquidity cycle analysis indicates 2025 will feature one of the most substantial global refinancing needs across private and public sector debt
- Potential positive market factors include:
  - Tax cuts
  - Increased mergers and acquisitions (M&A)
  - Potential Dogecoin developments
- Potential negative market factors include:
  - Tariffs
  - Strong US dollar
  - Potential stagflation
  - Potential lowering of the Federal Reserve's "put" price
- Investors are cautioned against simply extrapolating 2024 or post-election performance into future expectations
- Recommended approach: Develop high-probability scenarios for the size, sequence, and scope of global macro thematic changes
