Key Insight
Crypto technology can significantly reduce operational costs for financial services, potentially creating substantial efficiency gains and cost savings across traditional brokerage and trading platforms.

<list_of_important_information>
- Replacing brick-and-mortar infrastructure with software can dramatically lower operational expenses
- Robinhood reports crypto business operational costs are over 10 times lower than traditional brokerage operations
- Fundamental trading functionality remains similar across traditional and crypto platforms (asset swapping)
- Initial benefits of crypto technology adoption include improved company margins
- Long-term potential involves passing cost savings to end users through lower investing costs
</list_of_important_information>
